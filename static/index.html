Is CAC registration really free through SMEDAN?
SMEDAN-supported programs may cover CAC registration fees for eligible applicants and while program slots/funding are available. The course explains how to follow that pathway correctly.
What am I paying ₦5000 for?
You’re paying for the educational guide (step-by-step instructions, checklists, and updates) plus WhatsApp support. This is not an official government service.
Do you issue CAC certificates?
No. CAC certificates and approvals are issued by the official institutions. We provide guidance and support to help you complete the process properly.
How do I get access after payment?
After Paystack confirms your payment, you’ll see a success message and receive an access email (as configured on your backend). This page includes a production-ready Paystack frontend example.
What do I need before starting?
A valid email, a reachable phone number, and your business details (names/choices). The course includes a pre-checklist to keep you organized.
Is this suitable for complete beginners?
Yes. The steps are simplified, with explanations, screenshots guidance, and WhatsApp support to reduce overwhelm.
